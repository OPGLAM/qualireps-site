# Refund Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026

## Merchant of record

Purchases of QualiReps are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited, which acts as the merchant of record and authorized reseller. Your bank or card statement shows a charge from Paddle.com.

To request a refund, reply to the Paddle receipt email you received at purchase, or contact us at legal@qualireps.app. Include the email address used at checkout so we can locate your purchase.

## 14-day refund window

You can request a refund within 14 days of the purchase date, no questions asked. Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method.

## EU/EEA consumers — right of withdrawal

If you are a consumer in the EU or EEA, you have a statutory 14-day right of withdrawal for digital purchases.

QualiReps is digital content supplied immediately. By unlocking the application with your license key before the end of the 14-day withdrawal period, you expressly consent to the immediate supply of the digital content and acknowledge that you thereby lose your statutory right of withdrawal for that purchase. Until you unlock the application with the key, the right of withdrawal remains available.

## Refunded license keys

A license key from a refunded purchase is invalidated. Once a refund is issued, the key no longer unlocks the full version.
